Lasagna

Description

When my grandmother first made this lasagna for me, I was over the moon. Now, I have many fond memories of friday evenings eating lasagna with my granny.

It took me a long time to try to convince her to share the recipe with me. What ultimately changed her mind was me having kids of my own with whom I'd be able to share this wonderful lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
